Support

RedBoard is a private team management application for the Denison University Women's Basketball program. Access is by invite only through your coaching staff.

Getting help

For account access issues, contact your coach. For technical issues with the app, reach out to us directly:

Common issues

  • Can't log in — Use "Forgot password?" on the login screen or contact your coach to reset your credentials.
  • Missing stats or film — Content is published by coaching staff. If you expect to see something, ask your coach.
  • Wellness form not showing — Forms are activated daily by your coach. Check back later or contact your coach.
  • Can't upload a profile photo — Photos must be under 5 MB. Try a smaller image.
