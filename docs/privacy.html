Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 14, 2026

RedBoard ("the App") is operated by Arborik, a software development company. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights with respect to that data.

1. Who this app is for

RedBoard is a private, invite-only team management application for the Denison University Women's Basketball program. Access is restricted to coaching staff and registered players. The App is not available to the general public and does not accept open registration.

2. Data we collect

  • Account information: Your name and email address, provided by your coach when your account is created.
  • Profile information: Jersey number, position, class year, and profile photo (optional, set by you).
  • Athletic performance data: Statistics uploaded by coaching staff from practice and game sessions.
  • Wellness responses: Answers to daily wellness check-in forms created by coaching staff.
  • App activity: Which film clips you have viewed, for the purpose of tracking unread content.

3. How we use your data

  • To provide the core features of the App: displaying your stats, film, wellness forms, and playbook.
  • To allow coaching staff to monitor team wellness and performance trends.
  • To send you a password reset email if you request one.

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, or any purpose unrelated to the Denison WBB program.

4. Data storage and security

All data is stored in Supabase, a managed database platform hosted on Amazon Web Services infrastructure in the United States. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. Access is controlled by row-level security policies that ensure you can only access data relevant to your account.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties, except as necessary to operate the service (Supabase/AWS infrastructure). We do not share individual player data with other players.

6. Data retention

Your data is retained for the duration of your enrollment in the program. If your account is removed by a coach, all associated data — including your profile, stats, wellness responses, and film history — is permanently deleted.

7. Your rights

You may request the following at any time by contacting us:

  • Access to a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your account and all associated data

8. Children's privacy

The App is intended for college athletes (age 18+). We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under the age of 13. If you believe a minor's data has been collected in error, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
