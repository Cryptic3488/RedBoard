## Description (4000 chars max — ~580 words)

RedBoard is the all-in-one team app for Denison Women's Basketball — built to keep players connected to their coaches and focused on improvement, every single day.

**For Players**

Your Feed surfaces everything that matters. Game and practice stats appear the moment your coach publishes them, with trend indicators showing how you're tracking week over week. Film clips from Hudl arrive with your coach's notes attached, so you know exactly what to watch for. Wellness check-ins remind you to report in daily so your coaches can keep the whole team healthy.

Stats — Your personal performance data, organized by session. View your points, rebounds, assists, steals, and more alongside team averages. Track your progress toward coach-set goals with visual progress bars. See exactly where you stand.

Film — Every clip your coaching staff shares lives here. Hudl links open inline so you never leave the app. Personal film sent just to you is clearly marked.

Wellness — A quick daily check-in your coach creates to monitor how the team is feeling. Takes 60 seconds. Helps your coaches make better decisions about practice intensity and player health.

Playbook — Browse team plays organized by category. PDFs and diagrams are right there when you need them.

Profile — Set your jersey number, position, and class year. Update your photo. Everything in one place.

**For Coaching Staff**

RedBoard gives your staff a clean command center to manage the full team experience without leaving the app.

Roster — Add players, set positions and class years, assign roles. Everything a new player needs to get started.

Film Sharing — Post a Hudl link or upload a clip directly. Add coaching notes. Send to the whole team or target individual players.

Stats — Upload a CSV or XLSX from your stat software. RedBoard matches columns to players automatically, handles custom metrics, and lets you publish or keep as a draft. Add annotations to individual players for context.

Wellness — Build custom daily check-ins with rating scales, yes/no questions, and open responses. Review team-wide submissions in one grid. See who hasn't checked in.

Playbook — Upload plays as PDFs or images, organized by folder. Manage your entire playbook library from within the app.

All content is private and invite-only. Players see only what's relevant to them.

**Built for Denison WBB**

RedBoard was built specifically for the Denison University Women's Basketball program — a tight-knit team that deserves a tool as focused as they are.
